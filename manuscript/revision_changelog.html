XGBoost achieved the best overall forecasting performance at a 1-day prediction horizon (MAPE=18.0%, RMSE=8.10; eTable 8), with competitive performance at lag 0 (MAPE=19.4%) and lag 5 (20.3%). MLP was the best-performing model at the 5-day horizon (19.1%).
